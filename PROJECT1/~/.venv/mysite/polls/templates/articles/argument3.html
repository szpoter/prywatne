55m Building Rising in Pomorzany

Pomorzany, one of Szczecin's rapidly developing districts, is witnessing a significant transformation with the construction of a new 55-meter-high building. This structure, set to become a modern landmark, symbolizes the district's evolution from an industrial area into a vibrant urban space.

The 55-meter building, designed by renowned architects, will feature a blend of residential and commercial spaces. The lower floors are dedicated to retail outlets, cafes, and coworking spaces, fostering a dynamic community hub. Meanwhile, the upper floors will house luxury apartments offering panoramic views of Szczecin and the surrounding areas.

The building's eco-friendly design is a key highlight. Solar panels on the roof, rainwater harvesting systems, and energy-efficient lighting make it a model of sustainable urban development. Residents and visitors will also benefit from ample green spaces, including a rooftop garden and terraces that encourage outdoor activities.

The project is part of Szczecin's broader urban renewal initiative, which aims to create modern, livable spaces while preserving the city's unique character. Local officials have praised the investment, emphasizing its potential to attract businesses and enhance the quality of life for residents.

With construction progressing at a steady pace, the 55-meter building is expected to be completed by 2026. It will undoubtedly become a centerpiece of Pomorzany's transformation and a symbol of Szczecin's growth and modernization.
